Our Mission

Effortless Style, Romantic Details

At Paparazzi by Biz, we design women's fashion in Los Angeles with a focus on romantic details, quality fabrics, and effortless wearability. Our collections are crafted for the modern woman who values both style and versatility.

What started as a passion for beautiful, thoughtfully designed clothing has grown into a trusted name among fashion lovers across the country. Through our online store and social media, we connect with women who share our love for effortless, LA-inspired style.

Each piece is carefully designed to bring your customers the latest trends — from embroidered details and reversible jackets to coordinated sets and resort-ready looks — all at competitive great prices.
